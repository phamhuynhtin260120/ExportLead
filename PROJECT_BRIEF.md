# ExportLead - Project Brief

## 1. Tong quan du an

**ExportLead** la mot website/tool noi bo danh rieng cho nganh van chuyen hang hoa ra nuoc ngoai. Muc tieu dau tien cua du an la tu dong crawl du lieu cac cong ty co kha nang can dich vu logistics quoc te, dac biet la cac cong ty san xuat, xuat khau, OEM/ODM, hoac co dau hieu ban hang ra thi truong nuoc ngoai.

O giai doan dau, ExportLead khong phai la CRM lon hay nen tang marketing phuc tap. San pham se tap trung vao mot viec that chac:

> Moi sang hoac khi nguoi dung nhap URL, he thong tu dong crawl du lieu lead ve, loc trung, phan tich tin hieu xuat khau, luu thanh bang va cho xuat Excel/CSV.

Sau khi du lieu da on dinh, du an se mo rong sang email marketing, goi y loi chao, gioi thieu dich vu, follow-up va quan ly phan hoi khach hang.

## 2. Van de can giai quyet

Nguoi lam sales logistics quoc te thuong can tim cac doanh nghiep co nhu cau gui hang ra nuoc ngoai. Viec tim kiem thu cong ton nhieu thoi gian:

- Tim cong ty san xuat/xuat khau theo nganh.
- Mo tung website de lay email, phone, dia chi.
- Kiem tra cong ty co xuat khau hay khong.
- Ghi lai Excel.
- Loc trung du lieu.
- Chia nhom lead nao nen uu tien tiep can.

ExportLead se tu dong hoa cac viec nay de tao ra nguon lead hang ngay cho doi sales.

## 3. Doi tuong su dung

Nguoi dung chinh:

- Sales logistics quoc te.
- Forwarder.
- Cong ty van tai quoc te.
- Don vi cung cap sea freight, air freight, FCL/LCL, customs clearance, door-to-door, express.

Khach hang muc tieu ma he thong can tim:

- Cong ty san xuat co hang xuat khau.
- Cong ty co website ghi ro export/international market.
- Cong ty trong cac nganh co nhu cau van chuyen quoc te cao.
- Cong ty co email sales/export/info/logistics cong khai.

## 5. Tinh nang giai doan 1 - Auto Crawl Lead

### 5.1 Quan ly nguon crawl

Nguoi dung co the:

- Them mot URL nguon.
- Them nhieu URL cung luc.
- Luu URL de crawl lai moi sang.
- Bat/tat trang thai cua tung nguon.
- Gan tag nganh cho nguon, vi du: garment, seafood, furniture.

Vi du nguon:

- Danh ba doanh nghiep.
- Website hiep hoi nganh nghe.
- Danh sach hoi vien.
- Danh sach exhibitor cua hoi cho.
- Trang B2B directory.
- Trang ket qua tim kiem co danh sach cong ty.

### 5.2 Chay crawl thu cong

Nguoi dung nhap URL va bam `Start Crawl`.

He thong se:

- Tai noi dung trang.
- Tim cac link cong ty lien quan.
- Vao tung trang chi tiet neu co.
- Lay thong tin cong ty.
- Luu vao database.
- Ghi log ket qua crawl.

### 5.3 Chay crawl tu dong moi sang

He thong co scheduler chay dinh ky, vi du 7h hoac 8h sang moi ngay.

Moi lan chay:

- Lay danh sach source dang active.
- Dua tung source vao queue.
- Worker xu ly crawl nen.
- Luu lead moi.
- Cap nhat lead cu neu co thong tin moi.
- Bao cao so lead moi tim duoc trong ngay.

### 5.4 Chong trung du lieu

Can loc trung theo nhieu lop:

- Website domain.
- Email.
- So dien thoai.
- Ten cong ty da chuan hoa.
- Source URL.

Neu gap cong ty da ton tai:

- Khong tao lead moi trung lap.
- Chi cap nhat thong tin moi neu tot hon du lieu cu.
- Ghi them source moi vao lich su nguon.

### 5.5 Bang lead

Cac cot du lieu quan trong:

- Company name.
- Website.
- Email.
- Phone.
- Address.
- Province/Country.
- Industry.
- Product type.
- Export markets.
- Export signals.
- Possible shipping need.
- Recommended service.
- Lead score.
- Source URL.
- Crawl date.
- Last updated.
- Status.

### 5.6 Export Excel/CSV

Nguoi dung co the:

- Loc theo ngay crawl.
- Loc theo nganh.
- Loc lead co email/phone.
- Loc Hot/Warm/Cold.
- Tai file Excel hoac CSV.

## 7. Lead scoring

## 9. Cong nghe de xuat

### 9.1 Stack MVP

- Frontend/Web app: Next.js.
- Backend API: Next.js API routes hoac NestJS neu muon tach backend rieng.
- Crawler: Playwright + Cheerio.
- Database: PostgreSQL.
- Queue job: BullMQ.
- Redis: Redis local/VPS hoac Upstash Redis.
- Scheduler: BullMQ repeatable jobs hoac cron job.
- Export file: ExcelJS va CSV writer.
- Auth: NextAuth hoac Clerk.
- Deployment: VPS, Railway, Render, hoac Supabase + VPS worker.

### 9.2 Ly do chon stack

- Next.js giup lam dashboard nhanh.
- Playwright xu ly duoc cac website co JavaScript.
- Cheerio nhanh va nhe cho website HTML tinh.
- PostgreSQL ben vung cho du lieu lead, source va crawl log.
- BullMQ giup crawl nen, tranh lam treo web app.
- Redis phu hop cho queue va scheduler.
- Sau nay co the mo rong sang email marketing, AI scoring va CRM mini.

## 13. Crawler layers

### 13.1 Source crawler

Nhiem vu:

- Lay danh sach link tu URL nguon.
- Nhan dien link cong ty.
- Bo qua link khong lien quan.
- Dua link chi tiet vao queue.

### 13.2 Company crawler

Nhiem vu:

- Vao trang chi tiet cong ty.
- Lay ten cong ty, website, email, phone, dia chi.
- Tim link About, Products, Contact, Export, Markets.
- Crawl them cac trang quan trong trong cung domain neu can.

### 13.3 Extractor

Nhiem vu:

- Tach email bang regex.
- Tach phone bang regex va chuan hoa.
- Lay title, meta description.
- Lay noi dung text chinh.
- Tim dia chi va nganh hang.

### 13.4 Signal analyzer

Nhiem vu:

- Kiem tra keyword export/logistics.
- Kiem tra thi truong nuoc ngoai.
- Kiem tra chung chi.
- Doan product type.
- Doan shipping need.
- Tinh lead score.

## 16. Nguyen tac san pham

- Tap trung vao nganh van chuyen quoc te, khong lam crawler chung chung.
- Uu tien du lieu sach hon so luong lon nhung kem chat luong.
- Moi lead nen tra loi duoc cau hoi: "Cong ty nay co dang de sales logistics tiep can khong?"
- Giai doan dau chi nen semi-auto voi email marketing de tranh spam.
- Luon luu source URL de co the kiem chung.
- Khong crawl qua manh mot website; can co rate limit va retry hop ly.

## 17. Ghi chu tu cac trao doi truoc

Nhung diem da thong nhat:

- San pham danh rieng cho nganh van chuyen hang hoa ra nuoc ngoai.
- Giai doan dau se tap trung vao auto crawl lead.
- Website co the tu crawl moi sang.
- Nguoi dung co the cung cap URL de crawl ngay.
- Du lieu can luu thanh bang va xuat Excel/CSV.
- Can loc trung va cham diem lead.
- Can nhan dien tin hieu xuat khau, thi truong nuoc ngoai va nhu cau logistics.
- Sau nay se them tinh nang email marketing/gioi thieu dich vu.
- Email marketing nen bat dau bang co che semi-auto: he thong soan, nguoi dung duyet, roi moi gui.
